Voting Process

How to Vote

Follow these four simple steps to cast your vote. The entire process takes less than two minutes.

1
🛡️
Log In Securely
Sign in with your Student ID and a one-time email code — no password to remember.
2
📋
Review Nominees
Browse through all nominees running in the election to make an informed decision.
3
✅
Select Nominees
Select up to 10 nominees you want to vote for. You can re-select your choices anytime before submitting.
4
📤
Confirm & Submit
Review your selections and submit your ballot. You cannot vote again once submitted.
⚠️ Important: Each student can vote for up to 10 nominees. Once you submit, your vote is final. Choose wisely!
