Voting Process

How to Vote

Follow these four simple steps to cast your vote. The entire process takes less than two minutes.

1
🛡️
Log In Securely
Sign in with your student email and password through our encrypted authentication system.
2
📋
Review Candidates
Browse through all candidates, read their bios and slogans to make an informed decision.
3
✅
Cast Your Vote
Select one candidate per position. Take your time — make sure you are confident in your choices.
4
📤
Confirm & Submit
Review your selections and submit your ballot. You will receive a confirmation of your vote.
⚠️ Important: Each student gets one vote per candidate per position. Choose wisely!
